# Nutt Labs Circle Privacy Policy

*Effective Date: April 6, 2026*

---

## Who we are

Nutt Labs LLC ("Nutt Labs," "we," "us") operates Nutt Labs Circle. We are the data controller for personal data collected through the program.

**Contact:** privacy@nutt.io
**Mailing address:** 1661 West Ave, Miami Beach, FL 33139, USA

## What we collect

- **Account information** — your name and email address, provided at signup.
- **Payment information** — processed by Stripe; we receive transaction records.
- **Usage information** — aggregate, cookieless analytics about visits to the Nutt Labs Circle site.

We do not collect sensitive personal data, and we do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

## How we use it

We use your information only to:

- Deliver Nutt Labs Circle (access, workshops, updates, billing).
- Communicate with you about the program.
- Meet legal, tax, and security obligations.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising.

## Who processes it for us

We rely on a small number of service providers to run the program:

- **Stripe** — payment processing.
- **Brevo** — transactional and program emails.
- **Cloudflare Web Analytics** — aggregate, cookieless site analytics.
- **Membership platform** — account and content delivery. *[Platform name to be added before launch]*

## Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for the site to function. Our analytics are cookieless.

## International transfers

Nutt Labs LLC is based in the United States, and your data will be processed in the US. If you are in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, or Switzerland, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses and equivalent safeguards to protect transfers.

## How long we keep it

We retain your personal data for as long as your membership is active and afterward unless you ask us to delete it. You can request deletion at any time at privacy@nutt.io.

Stripe retains payment records independently for the period required by tax and financial regulations.

## Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or receive a portable copy of your personal data. You may also object to certain processing, withdraw consent where applicable, and lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority or state attorney general. We will not treat you differently for exercising these rights. To exercise any right, email privacy@nutt.io. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law.

## Legal basis (EU/UK residents)

We process your personal data on the following legal bases under the GDPR and UK GDPR:

- **Contract** — to provide the membership you signed up for.
- **Legal obligation** — to meet tax, accounting, and compliance duties.
- **Legitimate interests** — to secure the service, prevent fraud, and communicate with members about the program.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated by email or through the program and will apply prospectively once they take effect.
